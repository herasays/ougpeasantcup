The Peasant Cup is a tournament hosted by the OUG Server, a bored community who still plays Overwatch to this day.

Four teams made up of members dubbed “Peasants” will compete for to become “The Better Peasants”. This fight that we never actually wanted will happen all within 3 hours of the same day. This is a zero-budget thing — what do you expect?

What is at stake is their pride, honor, glory, and the beautiful prize of a doodle of 13 fake dollars (read: absolutely fake), plus a DIY printable plaque to honor their new and well-deserved title.
